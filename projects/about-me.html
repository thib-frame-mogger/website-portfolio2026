About Me

Background

Originally from Lille, France, I had moved to Paris to pursue my studies in design. I graduated from ENSAAMA in 2025, where my specialization was metal creation. I have since then been working on kicking off a small workshop back in Lille. My work is still in its early stages, but I am excited to see where it will take me. I am dedicated to exploring, expanding and improving my skills in design, and soon enough, I hope to be able to launch a small collection of my own.
If you want to know more about me, feel free to reach out! Don't hesitate to come back to this page in the future, as I will be updating it with more information about my work and projects.
