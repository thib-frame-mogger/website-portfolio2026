Bavière

Bavière is a thin-line, compact shelf and plant-pot stand. It can accommodate magazines. It is inspired by the Bavarian flag and the polar star (eight branches). Composed of only four sheets of steel, it is designed to be practical and economical to produce, requiring very few tools. The ornament on the contrary, is a long, painstaking process: every line and star are drawn by hand onto the varnish before being chemically etched.

Bavière invites the eyes to wander across its vast exterior surface; as new patches of light are caught, the stars appear and disappear, in a twinkling fashion.

It brings together extreme simplicity in form and assembly with a refined artisanal savoir-faire in its ornament
